♥ Get to Know Me! ♥

I am a first generation college graduate, with my Bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems. I am a member of the first black greek-letter sorority, Alpha Kappa Alpha, Sorority Inorporated.

I've loved playing with computers and designing since I was around 6 years old. Half of my school free time was spent in the computer lab exploring all things of the internet.

I aspire to become a Developer of some sort, I'm not 100% sure yet but know that I want to code because it is my passion.
